Canopy

Forest monitoring

Scale conservation intelligence across India’s forest landscapes.

Canopy fuses satellite vegetation signals with acoustic threat detection on one map — built for NGOs, researchers, and government GIS teams. Open source. PostGIS-native. Ready for pilot data.

Geospatial + acoustic

NDVI ingestion and forest listening units on a single alert model.

Auditable exports

CSV with fusion provenance for patrols and institutional review.

Pilot-ready MVP

Demo completed — seeking boundaries and NDVI data for India.

Context

Why this matters for India.

Patrol capacity is limited across vast reserves. Separate satellite and acoustic tools delay response. Canopy integrates with existing government and NGO GIS — it does not replace them.

Carbon & biodiversity

Forests under pressure from logging, fire, and encroachment.

Community guardians

Low-bandwidth, location-specific alerts for field teams.

NIC & state GIS

Built for geospatial workflows and institutional pilots.

The solution

Combine what satellites see with what forests hear.

Three pipelines — vegetation change, acoustic threats, and spatiotemporal fusion — produce prioritized alerts with linked provenance on export.

Satellite / NDVI

Vegetation loss and canopy change. CSV today; Sentinel & Bhuvan planned.

Acoustic AI

Chainsaw, gunshot, vehicle, fire crackle — research model in progress.

Fused alerts

Space + time scoring. Links acoustic and satellite event IDs.

Workflow

How Canopy works

01 Configure regions & geolocated sensors
02 Upload audio clips → acoustic alerts on map
03 Ingest NDVI CSV → satellite change events
04 Run fusion → prioritized fused alerts
05 Export CSV · alert lifecycle for patrols

Problem

Close the monitoring gap.

Opportunity: Fuse complementary signals with auditable provenance — fewer false positives than single-source monitoring.

Satellite-only

Cloud, seasonality, coarse timing — weak for “right now.”

Acoustic-only

Local signal without landscape context — more false alarms.

Separate dashboards

No shared lifecycle or field-ready export.

Core

Spatiotemporal fusion.

Match acoustic alert to nearest satellite change within 500 m and 14 days (configurable). Scored output in CSV export.

fusion_score = 0.45×acoustic + 0.35×severity + 0.10×confidence + 0.10×recurrence
